Enterprise Solutions

Algorism works with enterprises to deliver solutions that enable significant improvements in business performance, process optimisation and product/service innovation. We provide solutions across multiple industries but have special focus and expertise in delivering solutions for Financial Services (Banking, Insurance, Other FIs), Retail and Telecommunications sectors.

Digital Marketing & Distribution

Algorism recognises the shift towards digital channels as the predominant channel for customer engagement. This is especially true for our clients in the services industry (Financial Services, Telecommunications, and Retail distribution). Algorism provides end-to-end services from conception to implementation to help our clients achieve success with their digital transformation projects. We provide expert advice and support to help you build an appropriate digital transformation strategy and have partnered global leaders in digital marketing and omni-channel service distribution solutions to ensure successful implementation of your digital business strategy.

Business Intelligence & Big Data Analytics

The ability for companies to quickly assimilate, analyse and act on the vast amount of data collected via transactional systems, customer relationship management platforms, social media and other contact channels can make the difference between gaining loyal customers and losing the to competitors. Algorism assists enterprises leverage their data assets to generate timely and actionable insights that drive business performance. We move enterprises beyond traditional descriptive/historical reporting into the new field of predictive actionable intelligence leverage data from both structured (transactional) and unstructured/semi-structured (interactional) sources.

Income & Fee Optimization

Businesses are under significant pressures – increasing cost of doing business and reducing margins due to competition/regulation. Our income/fee optimisation solutions enable clients especially in the Financial Services industry to optimise the product pricing process leveraging insights from Algorism and our global partners with specialised intellectual property for relationship-based dynamic pricing and automated enforcement of pricing concessions/covenants to eliminate income leakages.
