Innovation Services

Algorism helps dynamic enterprises leverage emerging disruptive technologies to achieve the next wave of growth rather than become a victim of disruptive innovations within their industry. We work with our clients in a number of ways to gain the innovative edge including:

  • As Advisor/Mentor: providing ongoing insight and guidance on emerging technology disruptions within your industry and how you can leverage this to gain competitive edge. Our innovation advisory services also covers support to your organisation in building an innovation engine (in-house or hybrid).

  • As Broker/Facilitator: working with you to define your innovation goals and linking your organisation to the appropriate innovation hubs and technology disruptors.

  • As Incubator/Provider: helping to host/incubate your innovation programmes or design, develop and implement customised solutions to specific innovation challenges defined by your organisation.
