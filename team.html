Leadership Team

Rasheed Adegoke

Founder and Chief Executive Officer

Rasheed Adegoke is a technology strategist and business executive with hands-on expertise in the design and delivery of high-performance information technology and business process improvement solutions to address complex business problems.

His core competence are in Information Technology, Process Improvement, Systems Integration, Digital Business Strategy and Solutions Design, Business & Technology Strategic Planning, Operations Management, Project & Change Management, and Organizational Restructuring. He has over twenty four years experience working with successful businesses especially in Management Consulting, Software Development, Financial Services, and Oil and Gas industries.

He was Chief Information Officer for leading banks in Nigeria for the last sixteen years of his career successfully leading major IT transformation and business integration projects in those roles. His last two positions were at United Bank for Africa Plc where he was the Director, Group Information Technology between August 2013 and August 2015 and First Bank of Nigeria Limited where he was Chief Information Officer from February 2009 to July 2013.

Rasheed is a respected industry expert who has also served on a Presidential committee on National Broadband Strategy and the TechLaunchPad initiative of the Federal Ministry of Communication Technology.
